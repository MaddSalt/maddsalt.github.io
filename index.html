About Me

Welcome to maddsalt.com. My name is Maddox, I'm 17 years old and I have been programming for around 4 years now. When I was younger the first language I ever actually wrote code with was bash. I used to build calculators and little programs that would ask you how your day went. I had a ton of fun learning and designing different programs but eventually moved on to more complex and in depth languages. Around the age of 15 I picked up a passion for cyber security. I currently hold two Microsoft Technical Certifications in Networking and Security. I strive to make everything I design as secure and robust as possible while always keeping user privacy and security in mind. SaltCheck will be my first app that I publish and I can't wait for it to be ready to launch on both iOS & Android devices. To learn more about SaltCheck visit www.SaltCheck.app.

What I am Working On Currently:

User & Data Privacy

I take user and data privacy very seriously when building apps. All apps and software that I build will give each and every user full access to any information that the app collects or asks for. When building SaltCheck, I approached this complex issue by assigning each and every user a random unique identifier in order to separate your name and your data. When the app is launched, the only way to sign in and create an account will be either through Apple, Google or Facebook (I reccomend signing in with your Apple ID if you have one). This action ensures that each and every user won't have any revealing or personal informtion stored with SaltCheck and that their data will remain safe and secure. In today's time, privacy online is something I believe every human should have a right to. In following this belief, I strive to make sure I take every precaution necessary in order to keep your data safe. If you have any further question(s) about my upcoming app's (SaltCheck) privacy policy or questions regarding user data in general please feel free to use the contact form below. To learn more specifics about how SaltCheck will approach user privacy please view www.saltcheck.app as I will provide user privacy updates there in the future.
